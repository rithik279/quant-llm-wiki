# Intelligence Explosion

What happens after AGI: automated AI research leads to superintelligence.

## The Mechanism

1. **AGI automates AI research**
   - Hundreds of millions of AGIs could run simultaneously
   - 100M+ "researchers" vs ~10k at frontier labs
   - 24/7, no fatigue, instant replication of insights

2. **Algorithmic progress accelerates**
   - Normal: ~0.5 OOMs/year
   - With automated research: 5+ OOMs in 1 year
   - This is the "compression" of a decade into a year

3. **Recursion kicks in**
   - Better AI → faster AI research → even better AI
   - AlphaGo analogy: after learning from humans, AlphaGo self-played to superhuman levels
   - Same will happen with AI research

## Compute Bottleneck

Even 100M researchers face compute limits for experiments. But:
- Superhuman intuitions from training on millions of experiments
- "Yolo runs" — getting it right on first try based on intuition
- 10x more efficient than human researchers
- 1000x more thinking (and faster)

> "What I do know is that the people who can do this are surely 10-100x AI researchers." — Jason

## Timeline Implications

- **2026-27**: AGI achieved
- **2027-28**: Automated AI research begins, intelligence explosion starts
- **2028+**: Superintelligence — "vastly smarter than humans, capable of novel, creative, complicated behavior we couldn't even begin to understand"

## The Bomb and The Super Analogy

- **The Bomb** (atomic): efficient bombing campaign
- **The Super** (hydrogen): country-annihilating device
- Same progression: AGI → Superintelligence

> "The Bomb was a more efficient bombing campaign. The Super was a country-annihilating device."

## Implications for Leopold

The intelligence explosion accelerates:
1. Infrastructure buildout (more urgency, more capital)
2. Power requirements (more GPUs, more compute)
3. Timeline compression (everything happens faster than expected)

The bottlenecks become MORE acute, not less.
