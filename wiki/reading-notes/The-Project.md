# The Project

Government involvement in AGI: the national security state wakes up.

## Key Argument

> "No startup can handle superintelligence. Somewhere in a SCIF, the endgame will be on."

By 2027-28, the US government will have a government AGI project.

## Why Government Gets Involved

1. **Superintelligence = decisive economic/military advantage**
2. **National security implications** — "forces not seen in half a century"
3. **Race with CCP** — if we're unlucky, all-out war
4. **Startup limitations** — can't handle superintelligence's implications alone

## Implications for Leopold

Government involvement means:
- **More capital flowing into infrastructure** — government money is non-reactive
- **Acceleration of timelines** — national security urgency
- **Supply chains become matters of national security** — bottleneck thesis reinforced
- **Power, chips, networks become strategic assets**

## The Race with China

> "In the race to AGI, the free world's very survival will be at stake."

This sounds dramatic, but the implications for infrastructure investment are clear: unlimited budget mindset, accelerated procurement, strategic importance of every bottleneck company.
