# Agilith Strategy Priorities (Next 5)

More advanced alpha ideas from the Agilith research plan.

## 6. Narrative vs Numbers Divergence
**"Loud storytellers vs quiet executors"**

Buy companies that quietly deliver results. Short companies that talk big but don't deliver.

**Why**: Markets reward good stories in growth stocks, but over time results matter more than words. Hype gets punished if it doesn't convert.

## 7. Voice Stress Underreaction
**Thesis**: When transcript voices show unusual stress patterns, markets underreact to the information content.

**Signal**: Detect stress in tone, combine with negative content → short signal.

## 8. Analyst Revision Diffusion with Tone Gating
**Thesis**: When multiple analysts revise EPS estimates simultaneously (diffusion), combine with tone gating (if sentiment is unusually negative, the revision has more bite).

**Use**: Consensus vs individual revisions, track divergence.

## 9. Regime-Conditioned Sentiment and Attention
**Thesis**: Sentiment signals work differently in different regimes. Build regime-conditioned sentiment (bull/bear/neutral within RISK_ON/RISK_OFF/etc).

**Key insight**: Same headline = different signal depending on regime.

## 10. CPI and FOMC Scenario Second-Order Trade
**Thesis**: Predict how different CPI/FOMC outcomes affect not just rates but second-order effects (which sectors win/lose, duration, risk-off, etc).

**Use**: Scenario analysis before Fed meetings, position in second-order beneficiaries.

## How to Execute

Build research foundation modules first, then layer these as refinement signals.
