# Agilith Internship Overview

Key conversations and strategic direction for the Agilith Capital quantitative research internship.

## Key People

- **Patrick** — Fund manager, value-based investing, old school (30 years), wants to use AI/machines as opportunity
- **Aidan** — Patrick's son, working on AI processes, joining to help with processes in AI

## Core Thesis

"The real goal is: Build the ability to independently design, test, and validate trading signals."

## Three Research Streams

1. **Factor and Signal Research** — Testing which factors predict returns and under what conditions
2. **Market Regime Detection** — Classifying market environment for dynamic strategy allocation
3. **Portfolio Construction Framework** — Translating signals into portfolio allocations

## Key Discussions

- Strategy universe: Start narrow with momentum in growth stocks (semiconductors + supply chain)
- Second-order plays: packaging, power, cooling, uranium
- Regime detection: HMM for probabilistic state, Hurst for persistence, ADX for trend efficiency
- Monte Carlo simulation for forward-looking regime forecasting
- Options hypothesis: 95/5 split with LEAPs for convexity
