# Agilith Investment Thesis

Strategy for the Agilith call with Patrick.

## Call Flow Outline (30-40 min)

### Opening (3-5 min)
Let Aiden share what they have been thinking. Then: "I've been thinking about how to make this concrete. Let me walk you through how I'd scope the first 16 weeks."

### Middle (20-25 min)

1. **Strategy Universe** — Start narrow: momentum in growth stocks, specifically semiconductors + supply chain. Mention second-order plays (packaging, power, cooling, uranium) to show depth.

2. **Regime Detection** — Three signals:
   - HMM for probabilistic state
   - Hurst for persistence
   - ADX for trend efficiency
   Explain displacement/distance physics concept.

3. **Simulation** — Monte Carlo isn't just validation — it's forward-looking. Regime forecasting using HMM transition probabilities + scenario overlays.

4. **Options Hypothesis** — The differentiator. 95/5 split, convexity on high-conviction. Testable hypothesis with clear break-even analysis.

5. **Proof of Execution** — "I've already built a trend detector for gold using EMA curvature. Same signal logic applies. I can show you if useful."

### Logistics (5-10 min)
Wait for him to raise, or near end: "I want to make sure we're aligned on logistics — hours, timeline, comp. What were you thinking?"

2. **Regime Detection with HMM** — Probabilistic state classification

3. **Monte Carlo for Regime Forecasting** — Forward-looking scenario analysis
