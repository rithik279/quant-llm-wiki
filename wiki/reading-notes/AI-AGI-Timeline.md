# AI-AGI Timeline

Timeline projections for AGI and superintelligence based on OOM counting.

## Key Dates

| Year | Milestone | Key Indicator |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 2024 | Current | GPT-4 class models, early agent systems |
| 2025-26 | Pre-AGI surge | $100B clusters, 1GW power, models beating PhDs |
| 2026-27 | **AGI** | Models do AI researcher/engineer work |
| 2027-28 | Intelligence explosion | Automated AI research, recursive improvement |
| 2028+ | Superintelligence | Vastly superhuman AI, 100M+ AI researchers |

## The OOM Framework

From Situational Awareness — three drivers of progress:

1. **Compute** ~0.5 OOMs/year
2. **Algorithmic efficiencies** ~0.5 OOMs/year
3. **Unhobbling** (tools, agents, removing constraints)

Total: ~1 OOM/year consistent improvement.

## Intelligence Explosion Mechanism

1. AGI automate AI research
2. Hundreds of millions of AGIs running 24/7
3. 100M+ "researchers" vs ~10k human researchers at frontier labs
4. Compress 5+ OOMs algorithmic progress into 1 year
5. Rapidly go from human-level to vastly superhuman
