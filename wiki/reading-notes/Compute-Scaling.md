# Compute Scaling

Deep dive: how compute drives AI progress.

## The OOMs per Year

| Driver | OOMs/Year | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|

| **Total** | **~1 OOM/year** | Consistent trend for 15+ OOMs |
