# Security for AGI

The national security threat: labs treating AGI secrets like SaaS software.

## The Problem

> "The nation's leading AI labs treat security as an afterthought. Currently, they're basically handing the key secrets for AGI to the CCP on a silver platter."

Labs measure security against "random tech startups" not "key national defense projects."

## The Stakes

AGI secrets = most important US national defense secrets:
- B-21 bomber blueprints
- Columbia-class submarine blueprints
- "Nuclear secrets"

But treated like random SaaS software today.

## Two Attack Surfaces

### 1. Model Weights
AGI is "just a large file on a computer." Stolen weights = stolen AGI.
- Currently: few years to secure before AGI weights are critical
- Timeline pressure: AGI by 2027 means 12-24 months to fix security

### 2. Algorithmic Secrets
Key technical breakthroughs for AGI being developed NOW:
- RL / self-play
- Synthetic data
- "Next paradigm" after LLM

## The CCP Threat

State actors with formidable capabilities:
- "Zero-click hack any desired iPhone and Mac with just a phone number"
- Chinese Ministry of State Security bringing full force
- Labs barely able to defend against scriptkiddies

> "In the next 12-24 months, we will leak key AGI breakthroughs to the CCP. It will be the national security establishment's single greatest regret before the decade is out."

## Implications for Leopold

Security concerns don't directly affect infrastructure thesis, but:
- Accelerate government involvement (The Project)
- Potential supply chain restrictions
- National security treatment of bottleneck companies
