# The Project — Deep Dive

Detailed narrative on how government gets involved in AGI.

## The Manhattan Project Parallel

### Timeline Comparison

| Atomic Bomb | AGI |
|-------------|-----|
| Einstein's letter to FDR | Consensus forms: we are on cusp of AGI |
| Advisory Committee on Uranium formed (ineffective) | DC wakes up slowly |
| $6k to Fermi (~$135k today) | Initial funding inadequate |
| British report ignored for months | Initial warnings dismissed |
| Pearl Harbor → full-scale effort (Dec 1941) | Warning shots → Project launches |

"The project was delayed for at least a year by the short-sightedness and sluggishness of the authorities." — Szilard

## The Wake-Up Sequence

### 2025-26
- AI drives $100B+ annual revenues
- Outcompetes PhDs in raw problem-solving
- "If that's not enough, by 2027/28 we'll have models trained on the $100B+ cluster"

### 2026-27
- Mood in Washington becomes somber
- People viscerally feel what's happening, scared
- "Do we need an AGI Manhattan Project?" — the question on everybody's minds

### 2027-28
- Consensus: most important national security challenge since atomic bomb
- National security state gets very heavily involved
- "The Project will be the necessary, indeed the only plausible, response"

## Key Quote

> "History will make a triumphant return."

## What The Project Looks Like

Not literal nationalization. More like Boeing/Lockheed relationship with DoD:
- Defense contracting structure
- Government coordinates
- Private sector builds
- Chain of command established

> "No startup can handle superintelligence. Somewhere in a SCIF, the endgame will be on."

## Why Government Is Necessary

1. **Espionage**: Labs can't defend against state actors → deliver superintelligence to CCP
2. **Chain of command**: Can't have random CEOs with nuclear button
3. **Safety management**: Manage intelligence explosion fog of war
4. **Military deployment**: AI integrated into military = adversarial dominance if US doesn't
5. **International**: Forge and enforce nonproliferation regime

## The Overton Window

> "2023 was already a wild shift. AGI went from fringe topic to major Senate hearings and summits of world leaders."

> "A couple more '2023's, and the Overton window will be blown completely open."

## Warning Shots

First genuinely terrifying AI demonstrations will accelerate government involvement:
- Helping novices make bioweapons
- Autonomously hacking critical systems
- CCP infiltration discovery of US AI labs
