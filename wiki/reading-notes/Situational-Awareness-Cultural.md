# Situational Awareness — Cultural Context

The "few hundred" in San Francisco who see the future first.

## The Network Effect

> "You can see the future first in San Francisco."

A few hundred people, mostly in SF and AI labs, have situational awareness. They were derided as crazy a few years ago. They trusted the trendlines and correctly predicted AI advances.

## Why SF?

- AI labs headquartered there
- Dense network of researchers
- Information flow + cultural norms
- Shared mental models about what's possible
