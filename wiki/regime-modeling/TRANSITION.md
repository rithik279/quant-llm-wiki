# TRANSITION

Uncertainty regime: reduced conviction, watching for signal clarity.

## Conditions
- VIX 20-30
- OR Spreads 400-600 bps

## Position Size
**70%** — Reduce exposure, maintain presence

## Characteristics
- Elevated but not extreme fear
- Credit stress but not crisis
- Mixed signals on direction
- Time to be selective

## When Exiting TRANSITION

Exit to RISK_ON (back to 100%) when:
- VIX < 20 AND spreads < 400 bps

Exit to RISK_OFF when:
- VIX > 30 OR spreads > 600 bps

Exit to RECOVERY when:
- VIX declining from peak (even if still > 20)
