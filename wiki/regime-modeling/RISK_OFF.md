# RISK_OFF

Defensive regime: minimum exposure, preserve capital.

## Conditions
- VIX > 30
- OR Spreads > 600 bps

## Position Size
**20%** — Heavy defensive, preserve capital

## Characteristics
- High market fear
- Credit crisis / stress
- Risk assets sell off hard
- Correlation between all assets spikes
- No point fighting the tape

## When Exiting RISK_OFF

Exit to RECOVERY when:
- VIX declining from peak (even if still > 30)

Do NOT rush back to RISK_ON — wait for RECOVERY → TRANSITION → RISK_ON sequence.
