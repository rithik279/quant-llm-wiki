# RECOVERY

Post-crisis repositioning: partial exposure, early entry into risk assets.

## Conditions
- VIX declining from peak (even if still elevated)
- Spreads potentially still elevated

## Position Size
**50%** — Mid-conviction, partial repositioning

## Characteristics
- Fear subsiding
- Credit markets stabilizing
- Not clear direction yet
- Early accumulation of beaten-down assets

## Why RECOVERY Matters

Early repositioning catches the bounce. Missing RECOVERY means:
- Missing the best days of the recovery
- Buying at higher prices after missing the dip

## When Exiting RECOVERY

Upgrade to RISK_ON when:
- VIX < 20 AND spreads < 400 bps

Downgrade to RISK_OFF if:
- VIX starts rising again (false recovery)

Stay in TRANSITION if:
- VIX stuck 20-30, not declining
