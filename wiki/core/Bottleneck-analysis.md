# Bottleneck Analysis

The five infrastructure bottlenecks enabling the AI buildout.

## The Five Bottlenecks

### 1. Power
**What**: Electricity generation and grid infrastructure
**Why bottleneck**: "Fierce scramble to secure every power contract still available for the rest of the decade, every voltage transformer that can possibly be procured"
**Key stats**: 20%+ growth in US electricity production by 2030, 100GW for single training cluster
**Tickers to watch**: BE (Bloom Energy), VST, NEE, CCJ

### 2. Compute
**What**: GPU/TPU supply, chip fabrication
**Why bottleneck**: TSMC capacity, CoWoS packaging, HBM co-packaging
**Key stats**: 350k H100s (Meta), 1M+ GPUs by 2026 cluster
**Tickers**: NVDA, AMD, TSM

### 3. Memory
**What**: HBM (High Bandwidth Memory), DRAM
**Why bottleneck**: SK Hynix + Micron + Samsung垄断, HBM paired with GPUs
**Key stats**: HBM3e demand >> supply through 2025-26
**Tickers**: MU, SK Hynix (SKXZ), Samsung (SMSNF)

### 4. Storage
**What**: NAND, SSD, data center storage
**Why bottleneck**: AI training requires massive data storage, inference requires fast storage
**Key stats**: Data volumes exploding, enterprise SSD demand surge
**Tickers**: SNDK, WDC, Kioxia

### 5. Optical
**What**: Interconnects, fiber optics, photonic components
**Why bottleneck**: Bandwidth between GPUs at scale, latency requirements for distributed training
**Key stats**: Optical interconnect market growing rapidly for AI clusters
**Tickers**: LITE, NPTN, LRCX, Coherent (COHR)
