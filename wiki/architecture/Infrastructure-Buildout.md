# Infrastructure Buildout

The massive capital mobilization required for AGI: power plants, data centers, chip fabs.

## Scale of Investment

| Period | Annual AI Investment | Individual Cluster | Power per Cluster |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 2024 | $100B+ | ~$10B | 100 MW |
| 2026 | $300B+ | ~$100B | 1 GW |
| 2028 | $500B+ | ~$100s B | 10 GW |
| 2030 | $1T+ | ~$1T | 100 GW |

## Key Numbers from Situational Awareness

- **2024**: Zuck bought 350k H100s. Amazon bought 1GW datacenter campus next to nuclear plant.
- **2026**: Rumored 1GW, 1.4M H100-equivalent cluster in Kuwait
- **2028**: Microsoft/OpenAI rumored $100B cluster (ISS-comparable cost)
- **2030**: $1T cluster, 100GW power (>20% of US electricity)

## The Binding Constraints

Not willingness to spend — finding the infrastructure itself:
1. Power contracts
2. Voltage transformers
3. Land
4. Permitting
5. Datacenter construction capacity
